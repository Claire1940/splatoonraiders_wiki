## Equipment and Tank Customization

A major departure from the mainline series is the ability to customize your Ink Tank directly. In Splatoon Raiders, the tank is considered a core part of your outfit. 

> **Warning:** Changing your tank mid-mission is not possible. Ensure your tank configuration matches your team's needs before starting a co-op raid.

The 2026 gear system allows you to equip "Midair Jump" modules to any tank. This is a game-changer for platforming and dodging the aggressive mutated Salmonid attacks. You can also find tanks with built-in turret dispensers, allowing you to set up defensive perimeters without using your sub-weapon slot.

## Upgrade Paths and the Mechanic NPC

To get the most out of your gear, you must visit the Mechanic NPC in the central hub. Unlike the random rolls of previous games, Splatoon Raiders uses a more deterministic upgrade path. You collect "Power Cores" during raids and use them to unlock specific nodes on your gear's upgrade tree.

*   **Node 1:** Basic stat increase (Ink capacity, run speed, etc.)
*   **Node 2:** Utility slot (Added sub-resistance or faster respawn)
*   **Node 3:** Signature Perk (Unique to that specific outfit set)

This system ensures that your favorite **splatoon raiders outfits** remain viable throughout the entire 2026 campaign, as you can continuously pump resources into them to keep their stats competitive with late-game drops.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use my Splatoon 3 gear in Splatoon Raiders?**

A: While you cannot directly transfer gear pieces, having a Splatoon 3 save file on your console will unlock a "Veteran's Tee" and several classic hairstyles for use in your **splatoon raiders outfits** collection.

**Q: Are there any gender-locked outfits in the 2026 game?**

A: No. Splatoon Raiders continues the series' tradition of gender-neutral customization. All **splatoon raiders outfits**, hairstyles, and accessories can be worn by any character regardless of their chosen style.

**Q: How do I unlock the co-op exclusive "Raider" sets?**

A: These sets are earned by completing "Sector Bosses" in the co-op campaign. Each boss has a chance to drop a specific blueprint, which you can then craft at the Mechanic's shop using materials gathered from mutated Salmonids.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many outfits I can own?**

A: There is no hard limit on your wardrobe size. However, the game includes a "Favorites" system to help you quickly swap between your most-used **splatoon raiders outfits** before heading into a mission.
