## How to Change Your Hairstyle in 2026

Changing your look in Splatoon is a straightforward process, but many new players overlook how to access the full suite of **splatoon raiders hairstyles**. Unlike gear, which you buy at shops like Headspace or Man-o'-Wardrobe, hairstyles are part of your base character customization.

1.  **Open the Menu:** Press the **(+)** button while in the Splatsville or Inkopolis square.
2.  **Navigate to Options:** Scroll over to the "Other" tab.
3.  **Select Style:** Click on "Player Settings" to return to the character creator.
4.  **Species & Hair:** You can change your species (Inkling/Octoling) and then browse the updated 2026 hair gallery.
5.  **Confirm:** Save your changes to immediately see your new look in the lobby.

> **Warning:** Changing your hairstyle is free, but remember that some headgear items will drastically change how the hair looks. Large hats may "flatten" certain styles like the Afro or Double Buns.

## FAQ

**Q: Do certain splatoon raiders hairstyles give you a competitive advantage?**

A: No, hairstyles are strictly cosmetic. They do not alter your movement speed, ink capacity, or hitbox. However, some players find that shorter styles provide a clearer field of vision in handheld mode.

**Q: Can I unlock secret hairstyles through Salmon Run?**

A: While Salmon Run offers exclusive gear like the "Wave" themed suits, specific hairstyles are typically added through free seasonal updates rather than reward tracks. Always check the latest patch notes for newly added styles.

**Q: Why does my hair look different when I put on a hat?**

A: The game uses a "hat-fit" system. To prevent clipping, the game automatically adjusts certain **splatoon raiders hairstyles** (like the Afro or Buns) into a more compact form when a hat is equipped. To see your full hairstyle, try using "Earring" or "Glasses" type headgear instead.

**Q: Are Octoling hairstyles available for Inklings?**

A: No, hairstyles are species-specific. If you want to use the Tentacurl or the Punk style, you must change your character's species to Octoling in the settings menu.
