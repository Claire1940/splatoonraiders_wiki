## New Gameplay Mechanics: The "Raider" System

The core of the new experience revolves around the "Raider" system, a hybrid PvE and PvP mode that takes place in expansive, tropical environments. Unlike the traditional linear stages, these maps are sprawling islands where multiple teams compete to secure "Golden Zapfish" while fending off massive Salmonid bosses that roam the shoreline.

### Exploration and Mobility
Mobility has been overhauled to take advantage of the new controller's haptic feedback. Players can now "Ink-Dash" through the air, a mechanic that consumes a significant portion of the ink tank but allows for rapid traversal across gaps. This makes the verticality of the new tropical maps more accessible and strategic.

## Competitive Play and Ranked Overhaul

This system allows teams to register and compete in weekly events directly through the game's interface.

### New Ranked Modes
1. **Territory Control:** A permanent 12v12 mode where teams must hold three different points on a massive map to accumulate points.
2. **Extraction:** A high-stakes mode where players must find an artifact and reach an extraction point while being hunted by other teams.
3. **King of the Hill (Ink-Variant):** A moving hill that changes shape based on which team has covered it in more ink.
