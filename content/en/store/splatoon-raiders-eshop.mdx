## New Gameplay Mechanics: The "Raider" Archetypes

Unlike previous games where your "class" was determined solely by your weapon, *Splatoon Raiders* introduces a role-based system. When you download the game from the **splatoon raiders eshop**, you will have access to three primary archetypes that you can swap between matches. Each archetype has a specific role in the raiding process.

1.  **The Vanguard:** These are your frontline fighters. They specialize in high-output ink weapons like the Splatana and heavy Rollers. Their job is to breach the initial Salmonid defenses.
2.  **The Tech-Inker:** Focused on utility, these players use new sub-weapons to disable enemy turrets or create "Ink Bridges" for the team to cross gaps.
3.  **The Scout:** Equipped with high-mobility dualies and specialized scanners, Scouts are responsible for locating the Treasure Pods within the tropical ruins.
