## Understanding Cooperative Gameplay
Unlike previous entries in the series that focused heavily on competitive PvP, *Splatoon Raiders* shifts the spotlight onto cooperative exploration. Players take on the role of a mechanic, tasked with repairing an ark-like vessel to traverse a series of mysterious islands. Cooperation is not just a feature; it is the core of the progression loop. You and your friends must coordinate your movements, share resources, and combine your unique gadget abilities to overcome the increasingly difficult Salmonid waves.

### Core Co Op Mechanics
| Feature | Function | Strategic Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Bot Buddy** | AI-assisted companion | Provides unique combat abilities and traversal support |
| **Gadget Crafting** | Resource-based upgrades | Allows for specialized roles (e.g., turret support, AOE damage) |
| **Map Exploration** | Dynamic cloud clearing | Reveals new regions and hidden treasure chests |
| **Weapon Leveling** | Experience points | Unlocks deeper customization for your favorite gear |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always communicate with your teammates before spending your hard-earned Spiralite Shards. Upgrading a single gadget to its maximum potential is often more effective than spreading points across multiple weak abilities.

## Mastering the Bot Buddy System
The "Bot Buddy" is your most versatile tool in the game. Depending on which character you choose to accompany you—such as Shiver or Big Man—your bot buddy will provide distinct tactical advantages. For example, some buddies excel at crowd control, while others are better suited for boss damage. 

When playing in co op, ensure your team composition balances the different bot buddy abilities. If one player is using a "Killer Whale" variant for high burst damage, another should consider a support-focused bot that can deploy sentry turrets or provide defensive shielding.

## Gadget Crafting and Perk Customization
As you explore islands like Tiguro and Zapis, you will collect resources from defeated Salmonids. These materials are used to craft and tinker with your combat gadgets. Customization is deep, with 16 points available for allocation per gadget, allowing you to tailor your build to your preferred playstyle.

### Recommended Gadget Perks
| Perk Name | Effect | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Explosive Steps** | Deals AoE damage on jump | Clearing lesser foes |
| **Overpowered Attacker** | Boosts direct hit damage | Boss battles |
| **Emergency Damage Up** | Increases output when health is low | Last-stand survival |
| **Slash Area Up** | Increases hit radius | Crowd control |

## Tips for Efficient Island Raids
Successful raids require more than just quick reflexes. You must treat the map as a puzzle. The clouds that shroud the islands will dissipate as you explore, but you need to prioritize finding treasure chests and beacon points to unlock fast-travel routes. 

*   **Prioritize Objectives:** Don't get distracted by lesser Salmonids. Focus on the high-value targets like Big Shots or Steel Heads to secure the resources needed for higher-tier gadgets.
*   **Coordinate Specials:** Use your bot buddy’s special ability in tandem with your teammates. Chain-combining abilities can trivialize even the toughest boss encounters.
*   **Resource Management:** Keep an eye on your Spiralite Shards. These are essential for mid-game upgrades and purchasing rare equipment from the island shops.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Splatoon Raiders strictly co op, or can I play alone?**

A: While the game features a robust cooperative mode for friends, it is fully playable as a single-player experience. You can manage your bot buddy and tackle the story mode solo if you prefer.

**Q: How do I unlock new gadgets for my character?**

A: Gadgets are unlocked by defeating specific Salmonid types and collecting the required resources. Once you have enough materials, you can craft them at your base ark.

**Q: Does this Splatoon Raiders co op guide apply to local play?**

A: Yes, the mechanics for bot buddies, gadget crafting, and combat strategies remain consistent across both local and online cooperative sessions.

**Q: Will there be competitive PvP in this game?**

A: As of the 2026 trailer, the focus is entirely on a single-player and cooperative PVE experience. There has been no confirmation of competitive multiplayer modes like Turf War.
