## Strategies for Co-op Success

If you are planning to jump into the multiplayer aspects of Splatoon Raiders, keep these strategies in mind to maximize your efficiency:

1. **Balance Your Tanks:** Ensure at least one player is using a high-capacity tank for suppression, while another uses a high-mobility tank with the midair jump for objective retrieval.
2. **Utilize Turrets:** Don't treat turrets like sprinklers. Place them in elevated positions to cover your team's retreat or to pin down mutated salmonid spawners.
3. **Coordinate Upgrades:** Since the game features a larger-scale progression system, talk to your teammates about who is taking which "color" of upgrades to ensure you don't have overlapping weaknesses.
4. **Watch the Damage Numbers:** Use the visible damage feedback to identify which enemies are "tanky" and require focused fire from the whole squad.
