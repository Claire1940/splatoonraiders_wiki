## Navigating the Mutated Salmonid Threat

The primary antagonists in this adventure are the mutated salmonids. These creatures are more aggressive and varied than their counterparts in Salmon Run. They feature unique behavioral patterns that require players to adapt their strategies on the fly.

### Enemy Variety and Strategy

1.  **Swarmers:** These small, fast-moving salmonids attack in large numbers. Use the turret or high-rate-of-fire weapons to clear them out.
2.  **Armored Brutes:** These enemies require concentrated fire on specific weak points. In **splatoon raiders coop**, one player should draw aggro while others flank.
3.  **Aerial Mutants:** New flying variants that drop ink from above. Use the midair jump to reach their altitude and take them down quickly.
4.  **Colossal Bosses:** Massive encounters that often involve multi-stage battles. These bosses are designed with cooperative play in mind, featuring mechanics that require players to split up and activate environmental triggers.

> **Tip:** Always keep an eye on your teammate's ink levels. In cooperative play, you can ink the ground under a teammate to help them recover ink faster while they are in squid/octopus form.

*   **The Scout:** Utilizes the midair jump and lightweight weapons to collect resources and trigger objectives across the map.
