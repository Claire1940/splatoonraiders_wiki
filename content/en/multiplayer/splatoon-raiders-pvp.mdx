## New Movement Mechanics in Splatoon Raiders PvP

The most significant shift in gameplay for 2026 is the overhaul of movement. For the first time in the series, players have access to a dedicated double jump and enhanced mid-air maneuvers. In previous titles, verticality was limited to inkable walls and Squid Surges. In **splatoon raiders pvp**, the "Mirror Jump" and double jump mechanics allow for unprecedented aerial combat.

These movement options aren't just for show; they are integral to navigating the non-linear environments of Erp Island. In a competitive setting, being able to change direction mid-air or reach higher vantage points without needing a trail of ink will completely change how players approach Turf War and ranked modes.

| Movement Ability | Description | Tactical Advantage |

One of the biggest surprises for the 2026 launch is the heavy presence of Salmonids outside of the traditional Salmon Run mode. These enemies are now part of the main world, using "sludge ink" that appears more viscous and hazardous than standard ink.

In **splatoon raiders pvp**, these environmental hazards could act as a third-party force. Imagine a Turf War match where a "Fish Vulture" or a "Salmonid Lid" drops into the center of the map, forcing both teams to either cooperate or use the chaos to their advantage. This "PvPvE" element would be a first for the series and would require a total rethink of traditional strategies.

> **⚠️ Warning:** "Sludge" ink cannot be swam through and deals damage over time. Always keep a sub-weapon ready to clear a path if you get cornered by Salmonid enemies.
