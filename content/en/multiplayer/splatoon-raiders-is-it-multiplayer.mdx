## Splatoon Raiders Multiplayer and Co-op Functionality

Unlike the traditional Turf War or Anarchy Battles found in the main series, Splatoon Raiders focuses its multiplayer energy on cooperative play. Players can join forces to navigate complex levels, solve environmental puzzles, and take down massive bosses. This co-op integration is a first for a "single-player focused" Splatoon title, bridging the gap between the roguelike elements of Side Order and the team-based strategy of Salmon Run.

The multiplayer component allows for up to four players to drop into missions together. Each player takes on the role of a "Mechanic," utilizing specialized tanks and equipment that can be upgraded over time. The synergy between players is vital, as the difficulty scales based on the number of Inklings and Octolings in the session.

## New Gameplay Mechanics and Movement

One of the most exciting additions in 2026 is the revamped movement system. Every player, regardless of their tank choice, can now equip a **Midair Jump**. This adds a layer of verticality previously unseen in Splatoon campaigns. When asking "splatoon raiders is it multiplayer," it is important to note that these movement mechanics are fully synced across all players, allowing for coordinated aerial assaults.

The "Mechanic" role isn't just a title; it defines the gameplay. You aren't just shooting ink; you are managing a sophisticated tank system. The animations in Splatoon Raiders are noticeably more fluid than Splatoon 3, providing a high-fidelity experience that justifies its standalone nature.

## Equipment and Tank Progression

The "Tank" system replaces traditional weapon loadouts. Instead of just picking a Splattershot, you choose a base tank and modify it with various modules. This system is heavily inspired by the "Side Order" format but expanded into a full-length game.

1.  **Select Base Tank:** Choose between high mobility, high defense, or balanced stats.
2.  **Equip Modules:** Add the Midair Jump, Turrets, or specialized ink types.
3.  **Upgrade with Currency:** Spend resources gathered during missions to increase damage and ink efficiency.
4.  **Synchronize with Squad:** In multiplayer, ensure your tank modules complement your teammates' builds.

For players still asking **splatoon raiders is it multiplayer**, these deep RPG-lite elements are specifically designed to be shared. Experimenting with different tank combinations in a group of four is the intended way to experience the late-game content.
