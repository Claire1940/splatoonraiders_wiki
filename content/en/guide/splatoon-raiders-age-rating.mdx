## Gameplay Mechanics: Playing as a Mechanic

One of the most significant shifts in this spinoff is the protagonist's role. Instead of being a frontline soldier in a Turf War, you play as a skilled mechanic. This shift in perspective introduces new gameplay elements that influence the **splatoon raiders age rating** by focusing more on puzzle-solving and environmental interaction rather than constant combat.

Set in the mysterious Spirhalite Islands, players will work alongside the fan-favorite trio Deep Cut (Shiver, Frye, and Big Man). While there are still "splatting" elements involved, the focus on "Raiding" implies a more tactical, exploration-heavy experience.
