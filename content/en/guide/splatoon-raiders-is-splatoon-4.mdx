## Core Gameplay and Co-op Mechanics

One of the most significant reveals for this 2026 title is the inclusion of full cooperative play for the main campaign. Historically, Splatoon's single-player modes have been solitary affairs, with the exception of the Salmon Run mini-game. Splatoon Raiders changes this by allowing groups to tackle massive, sprawling levels together.

The gameplay loop appears to be a refined and expanded version of the "Side Order" DLC from the previous game. Players can equip different tanks that come with unique abilities, such as the midair jump, which can be equipped on all tank types. This level of verticality completely changes how players interact with the environment.

### New Combat Tools

Instead of just standard sub-weapons, players now have access to stationary and mobile utility tools. The most notable addition is the **Turret**, which provides sustained fire and area denial, functioning much more effectively than the traditional Sprinkler.

| Tool | Function | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Deployable Turret** | Automatic fire at nearby targets | Defending objectives |
| **Midair Jump** | Vertical boost while in ink form | Platforming and evasion |
| **Enhanced Tanks** | Increased ink capacity and buffs | Sustained combat in large zones |

> **💡 Tip:** Coordinate with your co-op partners to ensure at least one player is running a Turret-focused build to manage crowds while others focus on mobility and objective completion.

The threat in Splatoon Raiders is unlike anything seen in the Splatlands. The Salmonids have undergone significant mutations, resulting in larger, more aggressive, and mechanically complex enemies. ## FAQ

**Q: Is Splatoon Raiders a standalone game?**

A: Yes, Splatoon Raiders is a standalone title priced at $50 digitally. You do not need to own Splatoon 3 to play it, which is one reason why many fans feel **splatoon raiders is splatoon 4** in all but name.

**Q: Can I play Splatoon Raiders solo?**

A: Absolutely. While the game is designed with a heavy focus on co-op, the entire campaign can be completed solo. The difficulty scales based on the number of players in the session.

**Q: What are the new Octoling hairstyles?**

A: The game introduces several new styles, including longer flowing hair and more intricate braided designs that were highly requested by the community in previous entries.

**Q: Are there mutated Salmonids in this game?**

A: Yes, mutated Salmonids are the primary enemies. They feature new attack patterns, higher health pools, and unique abilities that require strategic use of the new turret and midair jump mechanics.
