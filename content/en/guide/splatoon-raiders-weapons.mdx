## Customizing and Upgrading Your Gear

A new feature in Splatoon Raiders is the "Parts Scavenging" system. By participating in matches and the single-player "Raider Run" mode, players can collect scraps to modify their weapons. While these modifications do not provide raw power upgrades (to keep the game balanced), they allow for fine-tuning of weapon behavior.

*   **Cooling Fans:** Reduces the overheat penalty on certain Raider-class weapons.
*   **High-Pressure Nozzles:** Increases ink velocity, making it harder for enemies to dodge shots at long range.
*   **Weighted Rollers:** Increases the ink coverage of a roll but increases the "wind-up" time for a flick.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Modifying a weapon often comes with a "Weight Penalty." Heavier weapons reduce your swim speed in ink, making you an easier target for enemy Chargers.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Raider-class variants of weapons?**

A: Raider-class variants are unlocked by reaching Level 15 and completing specific weapon-based challenges in the "Raider Run" single-player campaign. You can also purchase them from the weapon shop using "Scrap Metal" earned in multiplayer matches.

**Q: Are the splatoon raiders weapons balanced for competitive play?**

A: Nintendo has implemented a "Season Tuning" system for 2026. Every three months, weapon stats are adjusted based on win rates and community feedback to ensure that no single weapon dominates the meta.

**Q: Can I use my Splatoon 3 save data to get weapons early?**

A: While you cannot transfer weapons directly, having a Splatoon 3 save file on your Nintendo Switch 2 will grant you "Legacy Vouchers," which can be traded for three standard-class weapons of your choice at the start of the game.

**Q: What is the best weapon for beginners in Splatoon Raiders?**

A: The standard Splattershot remains the best entry point. However, for those looking to try the new mechanics, the **Scrap-Squeezer** offers a forgiving fire rate with decent ink coverage, making it one of the most accessible **splatoon raiders weapons** for new players.
