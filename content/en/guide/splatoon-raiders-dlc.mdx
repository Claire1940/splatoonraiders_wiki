The **Splatoon Raiders DLC** is more than just a map pack; it is a fundamental expansion of the game’s core mechanics. Set in the sun-drenched "Isle of Orizon," players are no longer confined to urban landscapes. This expansion introduces the "Raider" game mode, a 4-player cooperative experience that bridges the gap between Salmon Run and the traditional Story Mode. In these missions, players must infiltrate fortified Octarian outposts to recover "Primal Zapfish." Unlike previous modes, the Raiders DLC emphasizes specialized roles, requiring teams to balance heavy fire, ink coverage, and mobility.

## Deep Dive: Raid Infiltration Mechanics

The Raider mode is the centerpiece of the **Splatoon Raiders DLC**. In this mode, players must work through three distinct phases:

### Phase 1: The Breach
Teams must ink a specific path through an Octarian-guarded perimeter. Stealth is rewarded here; if you can reach the first checkpoint without being detected, you receive a "Surprise Bonus" that fills your special gauge immediately.

### Phase 2: The Collection
Players must find and carry three Power Eggs to a central harvester. Carrying an egg slows your movement and prevents you from using primary weapons, meaning your teammates must provide cover fire as you escort the objective.

### Phase 3: The Guardian Boss
Each raid ends with a battle against a massive mechanical sea creature. These bosses require coordinated attacks. For example, one player might need to distract the boss’s sensors while another uses the Harpoon to pull down its armor plating.

## Customization and Social Features

The "Orizon Resort" acts as the new social hub for the **Splatoon Raiders DLC**. Here, players can participate in mini-games like "Squid Surfing" or "Volley-Ink." These activities aren't just for fun—they earn you "Resort Coupons" that can be traded for exclusive cosmetic items, such as tropical shirts, floral headbands, and custom ink colors (like Neon Coral and Turquoise).

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Splatoon Raiders DLC available on the original Nintendo Switch?**

A: No, the **Splatoon Raiders DLC** is exclusive to the Nintendo Switch 2 (2026) to take advantage of the upgraded processing power required for the new liquid physics and larger raid maps.

**Q: Do I need to finish the main story mode to play the Raiders expansion?**

A: While it is recommended to familiarize yourself with the mechanics, the Raiders content can be accessed as soon as you reach Level 10 in multiplayer.

**Q: Can the new Harpoon weapon be used in standard Turf War?**

A: Yes, the Harpoon is a standard addition to the weapon roster and is fully playable in all multiplayer modes, including Turf War and Anarchy Battles.

**Q: Are there microtransactions in the Orizon Resort hub?**

A: No, all items in the Orizon Resort are earned through gameplay, participation in social mini-games, or by completing specific raid challenges within the expansion.
