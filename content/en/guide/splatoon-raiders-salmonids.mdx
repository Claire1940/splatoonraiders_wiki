## Combat Strategies for Boss Salmonids

Boss encounters in *Splatoon Raiders* are multi-phase affairs that often involve environmental hazards. When engaging **splatoon raiders salmonids** of the Boss variety, keep these strategies in mind:

*   **Phase 1: Shield Breaking:** Most bosses start with a sludge shield. Use Frye's drones to chip away at the shield while you focus on clearing the smaller Salmonid adds.
*   **Phase 2: Vulnerability Window:** Once the shield is down, the boss will usually perform a "Desperation Move." Use your double jump to avoid the AOE and strike the glowing weak point.
*   **Phase 3: Enrage:** At 25% health, bosses often move faster and leave trails of damaging sludge. This is the time to activate your special ability or call in a Deep Cut mech strike.
