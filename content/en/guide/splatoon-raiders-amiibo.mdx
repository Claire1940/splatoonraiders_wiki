## Gameplay Mechanics and Setting

*Splatoon Raiders* moves away from the traditional 4v4 turf war format, focusing instead on a rich, story-driven PvE experience. Players are dropped onto the Spirelight Islands, a beautiful but barren desert archipelago. As a Mechanic, your primary goal is to search for treasure and scavenge materials to build your own weapons and tools.

The Deep Cut trio isn't just there for flavor; they are active participants in the story and can even be utilized during combat encounters. The game’s focus on crafting means that the tools you build are essential for traversing the verticality of the islands and fending off the "familiar creatures" that have inhabited the desert.

## FAQ

**Q: When is the release date for the splatoon raiders amiibo?**

A: The amiibo trio is scheduled to release on July 23, 2026, the same day as the *Splatoon Raiders* game launch.

**Q: Do I need the amiibo to play multiplayer?**

A: No, the amiibo are optional collectibles that provide cosmetic rewards and convenience features like loadout saving. All multiplayer modes are accessible with the base game.

**Q: Is Splatoon Raiders a sequel to Splatoon 3?**

A: It is considered a story-focused spin-off set in the same universe, featuring the Deep Cut characters from *Splatoon 3*, but with entirely new PvE gameplay mechanics.

**Q: Will these amiibo work on the original Nintendo Switch?**

A: While the game is a Switch 2 exclusive, the amiibo themselves use standard NFC technology and should be compatible with any hardware that supports amiibo, though their specific rewards may be limited to *Splatoon Raiders*.
