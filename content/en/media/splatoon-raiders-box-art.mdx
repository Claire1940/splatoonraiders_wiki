## Gameplay Mechanics: Scavenging the Spirelight Islands

In *Splatoon Raiders*, players step into the boots of the Mechanic. Unlike the standard Inkling or Octoling soldiers, the Mechanic specializes in building and maintaining gear. This shift in protagonist mirrors the shift in gameplay; while ink-splatting remains core to the experience, the focus is now on PvE (Player vs. Environment) encounters and treasure hunting.

As you explore the Spirelight Islands, you will encounter familiar creatures and new threats. The Deep Cut trio acts as your primary support team, joining you in battles and helping navigate the treacherous terrain. The crafting system allows you to use materials found throughout the desert to create unique weapons and tools. This adds a layer of depth not found in the mainline series, where weapons are typically purchased from a shop rather than built from scratch.

### Key Gameplay Elements
1. **Weapon Crafting:** Use scrap metal and ink canisters to build specialized gear.
2. **Treasure Hunting:** Locate rare artifacts hidden within the Spirelight ruins.
3. **Deep Cut Assistance:** Shiver, Frye, and Big Man provide unique combat buffs.
4. **Environment Interaction:** Use ink to reveal hidden paths and solve environmental puzzles.

## Multiplayer Features and Difficulty Scaling

While *Splatoon Raiders* is a story-focused spin-off, it does not neglect the social aspect of the franchise. The game supports up to four players in both local and online co-op modes. You can invite friends to join your raid through an in-game radio system, allowing for seamless drop-in/drop-out play.

To ensure the experience remains challenging for all group sizes, Nintendo has implemented a dynamic difficulty scaling system. If you choose to go solo, the number of enemies and their health pools will be adjusted to make the mission manageable. Conversely, adding more players will increase the ferocity and unpredictability of the creatures you face. This "Salmon Run" style of scaling ensures that the PvE combat remains engaging regardless of how many friends you bring along.
