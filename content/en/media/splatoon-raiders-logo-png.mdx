## Strategy for Successful Raiding

To excel in this game, you must master the art of "Inking and Linking." This involves using your ink to create paths for your piloted robot, which can then link up with treasure caches to extract them.

1.  **Scout the Perimeter:** Use your Ink Grapple to reach high vantage points before committing your robot to a fight.
2.  **Manage Your Ink:** Unlike multiplayer matches, ink is a finite resource on some islands until you find replenishment stations.
3.  **Upgrade Strategically:** Don't just focus on damage; the "Hideout Ship Construction" upgrades allow you to stay on islands longer.
4.  **Watch the Physics:** The robots are heavy. If you try to jump across a gap with a full load of treasure, you might end up losing your haul in the water.

> **Warning:** Falling into deep water while piloting a robot will result in a "Sunk" status, forcing you to respawn at the ship and potentially lose any uncollected treasure on the field.
