## Deep Cut and the Splatoon Raiders Characters Lineup

Shiver’s gameplay focuses on precision and aquatic traversal. Utilizing her connection to Master Megalodontia, she can navigate both land and ink-submerged environments with lethal efficiency. Frye utilizes her mastery over eels to overwhelm enemies with sheer numbers. Her attacks are less about precision and more about area saturation. In Splatoon Raiders, Frye can summon a barrage of eels that track targets, making her devastating in tight corridors where enemies have nowhere to hide.

### Big Man: The Heavy Vanguard
Big Man serves as the "tank" of the group. His massive frame allows him to soak up damage, while his ability to create illusion clones confuses opponents. Big Man can also "surf" across ink trails at high speeds, providing a mobile shield for his smaller teammates.
