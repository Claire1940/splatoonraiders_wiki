## Gameplay Mechanics: Treasure Hunting and Island Raids

The core gameplay of Splatoon Raiders involves departing from your home base to explore a variety of islands. Each island is teeming with hostile creatures and hidden treasures. Unlike the "Salmon Run" mode, which focuses purely on survival, Raiders emphasizes the collection of parts and currency to bring back to your hideout.

### The Piloted Robot Mechanic

One of the most exciting revelations from the recent trailer is the introduction of piloted robots. These mechanical units can be controlled by the player to navigate hazardous terrain and fight off larger hordes of enemies. The robots appear to be highly customizable, allowing for different weapon attachments and mobility upgrades.

1. **Deployment:** Launch from your ship onto a target island.
2. **Combat:** Use ink-based weaponry and the new robot units to clear enemies.
3. **Extraction:** Collect treasure chests and return to the extraction point before the timer runs out.
4. **Upgrading:** Use collected parts at the Mechanic's Shack to improve your gear.
