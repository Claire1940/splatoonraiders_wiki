## Lore of the Spylight Islands: The Salmonid Connection

Players will encounter Salmonids that look "indigenous" to the area, wearing clothing made from local materials and junk. This environmental storytelling hints at why the Salmonids have been so aggressive in prior games—they may simply be protecting their homeland or looking for resources to bring back to the islands.

### Key Lore Locations
