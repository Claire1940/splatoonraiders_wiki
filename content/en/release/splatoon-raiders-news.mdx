## The "Raiders" Mechanics: A New Way to Play

While Turf War remains the heart of the series, the "Raiders" moniker introduces a objective-based extraction mode. In this mode, teams of four must navigate through neutral territory to secure "Golden Zapfish Remnants" and safely deliver them to an extraction point while fending off both enemy players and AI-controlled "Salmonid Raiders." 

This hybrid PvPvE approach requires a more tactical mindset than previous installments. Follow these strategic pillars to succeed in the new mode:

1. **Prioritize Stealth** — Use the new "Ink-Cloak" gear to move through enemy territory without appearing on the map.
2. **Resource Management** — Ink is not just for movement; it is a limited resource that powers your Raider-specific gadgets.
3. **High Ground Advantage** — New verticality features allow players to "Splat-Dash" off walls to reach sniping perches quickly.
