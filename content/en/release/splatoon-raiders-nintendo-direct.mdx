## New Gameplay Mechanics: What "Raiders" Means

The shift from "Splatoon 4" to *Splatoon Raiders* suggests a fundamental change in the gameplay loop. While the **splatoon raiders nintendo direct** confirmed that classic Turf War modes will return, the primary focus appears to be an extraction-style mode. Players must venture into dangerous, ink-soaked territories, collect valuable resources, and reach an extraction point before the tide comes in or rival teams intercept them.

This "Raider" mechanic introduces a higher level of risk and reward. Unlike the standard respawn-and-go mechanics of the past, losing a match in the Raider mode could mean losing the gear or "treasures" you collected during that run. This adds a layer of tension previously only seen in the Salmon Run mode.

> **💡 Tip:** In the new Raider mode, prioritize movement speed and ink efficiency over pure damage to ensure you can reach extraction points quickly when the timer runs low.
