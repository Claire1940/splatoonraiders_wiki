## Mastering スプラトゥーン レイダース マルチプレイ (Multiplayer)

The most anticipated feature of this release is undoubtedly the **スプラトゥーン レイダース マルチプレイ** mode. For the first time, the "Side Order" style of progression has been expanded into a full-scale cooperative experience. In this mode, up to four players can work together to clear floors, collect upgrades, and defeat massive bosses.

### Cooperative Synergy
In multiplayer, the game scales difficulty based on the number of participants. This means the mutated Salmonids will have higher health pools and more aggressive AI when you are playing with a full squad. Players must coordinate their "tanks" and "upgrades" to ensure the team has a balance of offensive power and defensive utility.

> **💡 Tip:** Communication is key. Ensure at least one player in your group equips the **Turret** upgrade to provide cover fire while others focus on objective completion.

### Upgrade Paths in Co-op
Unlike the solo campaign where you must be a jack-of-all-trades, the multiplayer environment allows for specialized roles. You can focus your "chips" and currency on specific stat boosts:

1.  **Damage Dealer:** Focus on damage numbers and fire rate.
2.  **Support/Engineer:** Prioritize turret duration and ink efficiency.
3.  **Mobility Scout:** Maximize midair jump height and swim speed.

## New Mechanics and Customization

Splatoon Raiders introduces several "quality of life" and gameplay enhancements that differentiate it from the standard Splatoon 3 experience. The animation quality has been noticeably bumped, making the movement of Inklings and Octolings feel more weightless and agile.

### The Midair Jump Upgrade
One of the most significant changes to the movement meta is the **Midair Jump**. This can be equipped on all tanks and allows for a second leap while in the air. This is not just a platforming tool; it is a vital combat maneuver to dodge the sweeping attacks of the new mutated bosses.

### Turrets vs. Sprinklers
In previous games, the Sprinkler was a passive sub-weapon used for turfing. In **スプラトゥーン レイダース マルチプレイ**, the **Turret** is a legitimate offensive powerhouse. It functions as a deployable companion that targets enemies automatically, allowing you to focus on movement and high-value targets.

## Progression and Replayability

The progression system in Splatoon Raiders is designed to keep players coming back. Much like a traditional roguelike, each "run" provides you with temporary power-ups that reset once the mission is over. However, the "Raider Points" you earn can be spent on permanent stat increases.

### Permanent Upgrades vs. Temporary Chips
*   **Permanent Upgrades:** These include base health increases, starting ink capacity, and unlocking new weapon classes for your runs.
*   **Temporary Chips:** These are found during a mission and can drastically change how your weapon functions, such as adding explosive properties to your shots or increasing your fire rate for every enemy defeated.

| Progression Element | Type | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ink Capacity** | **Permanent** | **Reduces downtime during reloads** |
| **Fire Rate Chip** | **Temporary** | **Increases DPS for a single run** |
| **New Hairstyles** | **Cosmetic** | **Personalizes your Inkling/Octoling** |
| **Special Charge** | **Permanent** | **Allows faster access to powerful specials** |

## FAQ

**Q: Is スプラトゥーン レイダース マルチプレイ a standalone game or DLC?**

A: It is a standalone digital title priced at $50, though it shares many assets and the engine with Splatoon 3. It functions as a massive, expanded experience focused on co-op and single-player content.

**Q: Can I play the multiplayer mode solo?**

A: Yes, the entire campaign can be played solo. The game features an AI-scaling system that adjusts the difficulty and enemy density if you choose to play without a squad.

**Q: Do the rewards from Raiders transfer to Splatoon 3?**

A: Yes, many cosmetic items, including the new hairstyles and exclusive gear sets unlocked via Amiibo or gameplay, can be used in the standard Splatoon 3 competitive modes.

**Q: How many players does the co-op mode support?**

A: The **スプラトゥーン レイダース マルチプレイ** mode supports up to four players in online cooperative play. Local wireless play is also supported for handheld sessions.
