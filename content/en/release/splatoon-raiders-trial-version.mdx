The climax of the **splatoon raiders trial version** is the fight against the Crushing Crustacean. This boss requires a three-phase strategy:

*   **Phase 1: Leg Weakpoints.** Use Rollers to clear the smaller enemies while Shooters focus on the glowing joints of the boss's legs.
*   **Phase 2: Shell Breach.** Once the boss is toppled, all players must concentrate fire on the central shell. This is the best time to use the "Ink-Link" tether for maximum damage.
*   **Phase 3: Enrage.** The boss will move faster and spread toxic purple ink. Use the Slosher’s Refresh Mist to keep the team healthy while finishing the final 10% of its health bar.
