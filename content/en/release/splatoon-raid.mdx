## Competitive Evolution: Series Weapon Power

One of the most significant changes coming to the *Splatoon 3* Anarchy Battle series is the introduction of "Series Weapon Power." This new stat is designed to provide a more granular look at player skill. Unlike the general rank, this stat tracks your effectiveness with specific weapons based on your win-loss ratio.

This data will be used in the matchmaking process to ensure that battles are as fair as possible. For example, if you are a master with the Splat Roller but only a beginner with the Charger, the game will attempt to match you against players with similar proficiency when you switch between them. This prevents "stomp" matches where a high-rank player tries out a new weapon and ruins the experience for lower-skilled opponents.

### How Series Weapon Power Works

1. **Weapon Tracking:** The game records your performance metrics every time you enter an Anarchy Battle.
2. **Win/Loss Ratio:** Your power level fluctuates based on your ability to secure victories with a specific kit.
3. **Matchmaking Integration:** The system prioritizes matching you with players who have a similar "Power" rating for their currently equipped weapon.
4. **Badges:** Reaching certain power milestones will unlock new, prestigious badges for your Splashtag.

## The Return of Urchin Underpass

Nostalgia is hitting hard in 2026 with the return of Urchin Underpass. Originally a fan-favorite from the first Splatoon game, this map has been meticulously rebuilt for *Splatoon 3*. While the layout remains faithful to the original, the developers have added modern touches to accommodate the newer movement mechanics like the Squid Surge and Squid Roll.

Urchin Underpass is known for its narrow side alleys and the wide-open central plaza, making it a perfect stage for both long-range chargers and close-quarters sloshers. Its return to the rotation is part of the June 12 update and will be available in both Turf War and Ranked modes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful in the central plaza of Urchin Underpass; the lack of cover makes you an easy target for the new Embers-branded Specials being introduced in the update.
