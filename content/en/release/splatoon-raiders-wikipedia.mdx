## New Gameplay Mechanics: The "Raider" System

Unlike previous entries that focused almost exclusively on 4v4 Turf War battles, Splatoon Raiders introduces a hybrid PvE/PvP mode that gives the game its name. In this mode, teams must navigate treacherous ocean environments to recover "Golden Barnacles" while fending off both AI-controlled creatures and rival player teams. This adds a layer of strategic depth that necessitates a mix of combat and environmental navigation.

The movement system has been overhauled to accommodate the tropical setting. Players can now utilize "Current Surfing," a mechanic that allows for high-speed travel through moving streams of ink. This is particularly useful in the new multi-layered maps where verticality plays a much larger role than in Splatoon 3.
