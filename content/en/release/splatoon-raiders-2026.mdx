## Strategies for Successful Raids

To excel in **splatoon raiders 2026**, teams must move beyond the "ink everything" mindset. Since the goal is extraction, timing is everything. You need to know when to engage a rival team and when to focus on the AI guardians protecting the loot.

1.  **Prioritize High Ground:** The tropical maps are designed with multiple layers. Controlling the canopy gives your Pathfinder a clear view of incoming threats.
2.  **Chain Your Specials:** Coordinate with your Technical class to drop a turret before the Vanguard initiates a push. 
3.  **Manage Ink Consumption:** In the raiding zones, ink-refill stations are sparse. Don't waste your tank on walls that don't provide a tactical advantage.
4.  **Use the Environment:** Many tropical plants react to ink. Some might bloom to provide cover, while others might release spores that obscure vision for both teams.
