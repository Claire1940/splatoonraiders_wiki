## New Core Gameplay: The Raider Mode

The namesake of the **splatoon raiders update** is the brand-new "Raider Mode." Unlike traditional modes where the goal is simply to cover the most ground, Raider Mode tasks teams of four with infiltrating neutral zones to secure "Golden Anchors" and successfully extracting them. This introduces a PvEvP element where players must contend with both rival Inklings and aggressive local wildlife.

> **💡 Tip:** In Raider Mode, ink management is more critical than ever. Don't waste your tank on walls unless you need a quick escape route; save it for the "Guardian" enemies protecting the Anchors.

### Raider Mode Objectives

1.  **Infiltration:** Teams drop into a large-scale map with multiple entry points.
2.  **Scavenging:** Collect ink-canisters and power eggs to buff your team's special gauges.
3.  **The Heist:** Secure the Golden Anchor located in the center of the "Vault."
4.  **Extraction:** Reach one of two extraction points while being tracked on the mini-map by the opposing team.

## Exploring the New Tropical Maps

The **splatoon raiders update** brings a change of scenery, moving away from the urban sprawl and into the "Azure Archipelago." These maps feature significantly more verticality and water hazards. Since water is traditionally lethal to Inklings, the introduction of "Ink-Buoys" allows for temporary platforms to be created over the ocean.

### Map Rotation Overview

*   **Coral Canyon:** A multi-layered map featuring deep trenches and high coral peaks. Perfect for Chargers and long-range pressure.
*   **Sunken Shipyard:** A rusted industrial zone partially submerged. The tide rises every two minutes, cutting off lower paths.
*   **Palm Palace:** A luxury resort overrun by greenery. Features many tight corridors and indoor-outdoor transitions.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Falling into deep water still results in an immediate respawn. Watch your step on the Sunken Shipyard map as the tide changes rapidly!
